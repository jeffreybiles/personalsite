Get addicted to math

I've spent countless hours hooked into videogames, unable to pull myself away. If you haven't experienced that, it's likely your kids have.

What if they could spend some of that time learning math?

I've studied what makes games addicting, and I'm putting that knowledge to use in order to make math a mesmerizing experience.

Fully integrated math gameplay

I've been a gamer and a math tutor. Many people making educational games have only been one or the other. Without knowledge of both games and learning, you'll end up with a product that doesn't work. Gamers usually make a game that doesn't really teach math, while math tutors usually make a game that just isn't that fun.

In Math Monsters, the math is the core of the game. Players fight with pet monsters whose power goes up as you practice and as you learn new math skills. The harder the math skill, the more powerful the attack!

So while the player is motivated to learn harder skills, they are allowed to learn at their own pace. This makes for a learning process that is both relaxing and exciting!
