Electras

A game meant to teach kids the basics of circuits. It also doubles as a fun puzzler.

This one was a team effort, led by Carl Burch of Hendrix College. My part was in level design, tutorial creation/display, and internationalization support, as well as consulting and troubleshooting other parts of the program.
